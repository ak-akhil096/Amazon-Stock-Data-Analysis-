📊 Amazon Stock Data Analysis – Project Summary
🔹 1. Objective

The main goal of this project is to analyze Amazon stock performance trends over months, focusing on:

Price movement (Adjusted Close)
Trading activity (Volume)
Seasonal trends and patterns
🔹 2. Data Understanding

The dataset contains monthly aggregated stock metrics, including:

Average Open, High, Low, Close prices
Adjusted Close price (used for analysis)
Trading Volume

⚠️ Note: The dataset was initially unclean (pivot-style, missing values, unnamed columns) and required preprocessing.

🔹 3. Data Cleaning & Preparation

Steps performed:

Removed unnecessary/empty columns (Unnamed)
Extracted month-wise records
Converted string values → numeric format
Selected key features:
Month
Average Adjusted Close
Volume
🔹 4. Key Insights
📈 Stock Price Trends (Avg Adjusted Close)
Highest Price: July (~47.94)
Lowest Price: October (~40.88)
Strong upward trend from March → July
Decline observed from August → October
Slight recovery towards December

👉 Interpretation:

Mid-year shows strong bullish momentum
Q4 shows market correction / consolidation
📊 Trading Volume Trends
Highest Volume: October (~149M)
Lowest Volume: August (~109M)
Volume spikes in:
January
July
October–November

👉 Interpretation:

High volume + price drop (Oct) = possible selling pressure
High volume + rising price (July) = strong buying interest
🔄 Price vs Volume Relationship
July: High price + high volume → Strong bullish phase
October: Low price + high volume → Possible panic selling
August: High price + low volume → Weak momentum

👉 Insight:

Volume confirms strength or weakness of price trends
🔹 5. Seasonal Pattern Analysis
Q1 (Jan–Mar): Moderate, stable growth
Q2 (Apr–Jun): Gradual increase
Q3 (Jul–Sep): Peak performance
Q4 (Oct–Dec): Decline and recovery

👉 Conclusion:

Amazon stock shows mid-year strength and late-year volatility
🔹 6. Business Interpretation
High activity periods may align with:
Earnings reports
Market speculation
Global economic events
Q4 dip could be due to:
Profit booking
Market corrections
🔹 7. Conclusion
Amazon stock exhibits clear cyclical behavior
Best performance: Mid-year (especially July)
Weak phase: Early Q4 (October dip)
Volume analysis helps confirm market sentiment
